Package-driven development

A Golem is a package that includes a Shiny Application. Whether the interactive application is the core of the development or a tool for your package, {golem} will help you build a production-ready Shiny app.

Customisation

{golemverse} includes multiple packages around {golem} to help you customize your Golem.

Documentation

This website links to documentation of the different packages of the {golemverse}. Come back here each time you have interrogations about the development of your {golem}.
